## Common SDR Use Cases

### 1. LinkedIn Lead Generation

Extract qualified leads from LinkedIn based on specific criteria:

**What You Can Do:**
- Search LinkedIn by job title, company size, industry, and location
- Extract contact details, work history, and education
- Build targeted lists for specific campaigns
- Monitor job changes for warm outreach opportunities

**Example Workflow:**
1. Use LinkedIn People Search Scraper
2. Input search criteria (e.g., "VP of Sales" in tech companies, 50-500 employees)
3. Extract profiles with email patterns
4. Export to CSV and import to your CRM

**Actor to Use:** LinkedIn Profile Scraper, LinkedIn Company Scraper

### 2. Email Finding & Verification

Enrich your lead lists with verified email addresses:

**What You Can Do:**
- Find corporate email addresses using name + domain patterns
- Verify email deliverability
- Catch-all detection to avoid bounces
- Personal vs. generic email identification

**Example Workflow:**
1. Start with company domain and employee name
2. Run email pattern finder
3. Verify emails for deliverability
4. Update CRM with verified emails

**Actor to Use:** Hunter.io Integration, Email Verification Actor

### 3. Company Research & Enrichment

Build comprehensive company profiles automatically:

**What You Can Do:**
- Extract company info from websites
- Gather technology stack information
- Pull funding and financial data
- Monitor company news and announcements

**Example Workflow:**
1. Input company website URL
2. Extract key data points (size, location, tech stack)
3. Enrich with Crunchbase/Clearbit data
4. Create company profiles in CRM

**Actor to Use:** Web Scraper, BuiltWith Technology Scraper

### 4. Social Media Intelligence

Monitor prospects' social media activity:

**What You Can Do:**
- Track Twitter/X activity of key decision-makers
- Monitor company social media updates
- Identify engagement opportunities
- Discover conversation starters

**Example Workflow:**
1. Create list of prospect Twitter handles
2. Monitor their recent tweets and interactions
3. Get alerts on specific keywords or topics
4. Engage with relevant content

**Actor to Use:** Twitter Scraper, Instagram Profile Scraper

### 5. Event Attendee Scraping

Build targeted lists from industry events:

**What You Can Do:**
- Scrape event websites for attendee lists
- Extract speaker and sponsor information
- Get attendee LinkedIn profiles
- Build pre-event outreach lists

**Example Workflow:**
1. Find upcoming industry conferences
2. Scrape attendee/speaker lists
3. Enrich with contact information
4. Schedule pre-event outreach

**Actor to Use:** Custom scraper for event websites

### 6. Google Maps Lead Generation

Find local businesses in specific categories:

**What You Can Do:**
- Extract business listings by category and location
- Get phone numbers, websites, and addresses
- Find reviews and ratings
- Identify new business opportunities

**Example Workflow:**
1. Search "Dental Clinics in Los Angeles"
2. Extract all listings with contact info
3. Filter by rating and review count
4. Create targeted local outreach campaign

**Actor to Use:** Google Maps Scraper

## Best Practices for SDRs

### Data Quality
- **Verify emails** before importing to avoid bounces
- **Deduplicate** against existing CRM records
- **Validate phone numbers** with correct formatting
- **Check LinkedIn URLs** for accuracy

### Compliance
- **GDPR/CCPA**: Only scrape publicly available data
- **CAN-SPAM**: Verify email consent requirements
- **LinkedIn ToS**: Use data responsibly
- **Industry regulations**: Follow your industry's rules

### Efficiency Tips
- **Schedule actors** to run overnight
- **Use webhooks** to trigger CRM updates
- **Set up alerts** for high-priority leads
- **Batch processing** for large lists

### Personalization at Scale
- Extract 3-5 data points per lead
- Use company news for timely outreach
- Reference recent LinkedIn activity
- Mention mutual connections or interests

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Low Email Find Rate**
- Solution: Use multiple email finders, try pattern matching
- Combine personal and corporate domains

**LinkedIn Scraper Blocked**
- Solution: Use residential proxies, reduce rate
- Implement random delays between requests

**CRM Import Errors**
- Solution: Validate data format, check required fields
- Use mapping templates for consistent imports

**Duplicate Leads**
- Solution: Implement deduplication logic
- Check against CRM before import
