# Guides

Comprehensive guides to help you get the most out of Apify, whether you're a sales professional, entrepreneur, or developer.

## For Specific Roles

### [Apify for SDRs](/guides/apify-for-sdrs)
Learn how to supercharge your sales development process with automated lead generation, email finding, and competitive intelligence. Perfect for sales professionals looking to scale their prospecting efforts.

**What you'll learn:**
- LinkedIn lead generation at scale
- Email finding and verification
- Company research automation
- Social media intelligence
- Daily prospecting workflows

### [Apify for Bootstrapped Founders](/guides/apify-for-founders)
Discover how to replace expensive enterprise tools with cost-effective Apify automation. Learn to validate ideas, generate leads, and conduct market research on a startup budget.

**What you'll learn:**
- Market research and validation
- Budget-friendly lead generation
- Competitive intelligence strategies
- Content and SEO research
- ROI optimization techniques

## Getting Started

New to Apify? Start here:

1. Read [What is Apify?](/what-is-apify) to understand the platform
2. Choose a guide based on your role or use case
3. Try a pre-built actor from the [Apify Store](https://apify.com/store)
4. Build your first custom actor with our templates
